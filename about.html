About Us

Since the first iPhone in 2007, we’ve been in the iPhone business.

We even still have an iPhone 1 on hand and photos of the iPhone 3Gs shipment from 2009 (see below).

Ready stock for global shipment

In the early days of the iPhone, all markets were focused on new iPhones. At that time, Steve Jobs humbly said that he hoped the iPhone could achieve just 1% market share someday. Later, we all witnessed the incredible success of the iPhone.

Today, iPhones help us in many ways every day, and pre-owned iPhones can reach even more people. The pre-owned iPhone market is booming, and we look forward to cooperating with long-term partners to create win–win opportunities that benefit people and the environment. Together, we are excited to achieve great success!
